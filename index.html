Welcome to Our Auto Body Repair Shop!

About Us

For the past decade, Ghenadiy's Auto Body Shop has proudly served the Federal Way area. As business owners, we believe that we can only succeed by providing good and honest services to our customers for a price that won't break the bank. Our cars are essential in our day-to-day lives, so we understand the struggle of picking the right auto body shop. Rest assured, we have your back! Family-owned by the titular Ghenadiy and his son-in-law Timur, both of whom are first-generation Russian immigrants.

Frequently Asked Questions

What services does your auto body shop offer?

At Ghenadiy's, we offer a wide selection of services such as collision repair, dent removal, paint restoration, frame straightening, and more! Our expert technicians are equipped to handle any of your auto body repair needs.


How long will repairs take?

The amount of time depends on the severity of damage and the service that's required. Our team will give a rough estimate from the beginning and will inform clients about progress as they work. Above all, we strive to complete repairs on time without compromising quality.


How do I schedule an appointment for repairs?

To schedule appointments, please call our shop during our operating hours! Our staff will do their best to figure out a convenient time for repairs. (If the shop is closed, leave a message!)


Do you provide free estimates?

Yes! To get a free estimate, you may either bring your vehicle to our shop during our operating hours OR fill out the online form below. Regardless of the method, we will assess the damage and provide a detailed write-up for the repair costs.


Do you offer a warranty on repairs?

Yes, we have a warranty for all repairs! To inquire more about this, please give us a call.
